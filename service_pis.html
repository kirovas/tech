Firewalls & Network Intrusions

Firewalls are the first line of defense for any network. Acting as "castle walls" to protect your information, they permit only the traffic you allow into your office, making your office network safe and secure. We recommend that every office with an Internet connection regardless of the size had a firewall.

Network Intrusion provides defense against unauthorized devices being added to your local network without you knowing. Network intrusion works within the firewall, protecting against unauthorized users plugging in to the network behind the firewall. It is especially important for large environments with many devices coming on and off the network on a daily basis.
